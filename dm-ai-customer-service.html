AI Case Study

From “DM for Orders” to AI-Enabled Customer Service

Improving response time and customer experience using AI in real South African businesses

🧠 Overview

In many South African small businesses, customer engagement often starts with a simple phrase: “DM for orders”.

While this model works, it becomes difficult to manage as demand grows. This project explores how AI can be introduced to improve response time, consistency, and overall customer experience.

🚨 Problem

  • High volume of repetitive customer messages
  • Slow response times during busy periods
  • Manual typing of the same responses
  • Missed sales due to delayed replies

⚙️ Solution

I designed a simple AI-assisted workflow where AI generates suggested replies to customer enquiries, allowing the business owner to review and send responses quickly.

🔄 Workflow

Customer Message
↓
AI Generates Reply
↓
Owner Reviews
↓
Send to Customer

💬 Example

Customer: Hi, how much is this?

AI Suggestion:
"Hi 😊 The price is R250. We currently have stock available and we are based in Johannesburg. Let me know if you'd like to place an order."

📊 Impact

  • Faster response times
  • Reduced manual effort
  • Improved consistency
  • Better customer experience
